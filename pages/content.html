05.02.01 “Foodsharing Copenhagen”

Focus

The goal of the project was to create a campaign website with a campaign video for “Food Sharing Copenhagen”. Even though the theme is basic content we had to use our skills we gained through the semester regarding design, user experience and layout. We chose to make an educational campaign to reflect people how important it is to reduce food waist and how big is the effect on the environment. On the website we had the video we made called “The leftovers” that follows through a journey of bananas to the consumers plate while sharing easy and simple tips that encourage the viewer to take the next step for reducing food waste. On the website we also have recipes examples of easy cooking dishes you can make out of “old” food.

My Part

It was very important for me to make an effective campaign and so as for my group. We brainstorm through a lot of ideas till eventually we decided to focus on a simple video with a strong message. I took a main part in filming, directing and editing the video. I also helped to refine the website and its content so eventually I added copywriting and made it responsive.

Achievements

Through the process of the project I achieved many skills and competencies, for example: I Learned how to use adobe premiere in a professional way, I learned how to build a story in a dramaturgy frame work and translate it to specific scenes and then to a complete film.

05.02.03 “Storyboard”

Focus

To create a well-organized campaign film, we had to draw a storyboard so everyone in our group would be able to know what to expect before we start the production. We had to draw the different scenes with a small description and information about the angles, locations, props, sounds and composition.

Feedback and Improvments

Our storyboard got approved though we didn’t fill up the content section of each shot. We had a basic agreement of what we were supposed to shoot and what kind of locations and props we need. As for improvement we should have filled up the content section concerning all the things we needed to make a professional film production.

05.02.04 “Customer pitch”

Focus

At the end of the project we had to prepper a costume pitch for “Food Sharing Copenhagen” so we could present our main idea and values in a convincing way. We structured a three-parts pitch starting with “why” we do it. we motivate our listeners by presented a metaphor of the “ripple effect” that explain how small actions can make a major effect. Next, we explained “how” we encourage the viewer to take small steps to reduce food waist through our video. We presented some information and dramatic facts about the effect of food waist on the environment and how we could change it. Last we presented “what” we did. We showed our campaign website and its content and then we showed the video.

Gain

The costume pitch assignment taught me a lot about how to produce a meaningful and productive pitch. Through the process I gained skills of writing a convincing and motivating content directed to the target audience. I managed to talk in front of audience about the main ideas without having a written text with me.

Feedback

Surprisingly the feedback we got from our teachers and the board of “Food Sharing” was very good. They told us that our pitch had a good flow and our message was powerful. One point we got, challenged our steps/tips claiming that it is not so easy for certain people to change their behaviour, for example: it won’t be easy for an unorganized person to make a weekly food plan.
