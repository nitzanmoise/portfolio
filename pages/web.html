BASIC WEB

02.04.01 Responsive website

Focus

In the theme basic web, we learned different design principles such as: Gestalt principles, contrast types, and colour theory. Our last and most important assignment was the "responsive website" which we made in order to practice what we learned in the theme and prove the skills we gained.

Design

To create my website clear and organized I used some of the Gestalt principles in my design so it will be easy for the viewer to read, navigate and understand the different sections in the website. I also used colour contrast between the text and the background – white on black and black on white. I also used contrast between the size of the headlines and the reading text. I chose a complementary combination of colour grading from the colour wheel: brown and blue hues.

Gestalt principles I used:

  • Proximity of headlines reading text and images that appear next to each other.
  • Common region for text and headlines in a black background box.
  • Similarity of menu items with the same colour and changed colour while hovering.
Content

As part of learning how to produce content for the website I made a desk research about the Dada movement and art style, I collected different articles from the internet about the artists and the art pieces. I also collected images in different sizes. I picked the most important and interesting content for the website and divided the articles to sections: Heading and intro text with the main message of the Dada movement, sub headline with background and sub headlines with more details about the artists and the art pieces.

Programming and Layout

The website was build with HTML as the skeleton of the website and CSS as the stylesheet. I used an overall grid on the body and flex-box for articles and sections. In order to make the website responsive I defined three media queries break points starts with mobile first for small screens, then tablet and medium screens and last, desk-top version and larger screens. The layout of the website is symmetrical and “Mostly Fluid” so it changes in different break points for example: The main content turns from a row layout in medium and large screens to a column layout in small screens. The white space gets smaller in medium and small screens break points. The images and the text are also getting smaller in a fully responsive way because they are set in a percentage measurements than in pixels.

Gain

After compliting the assignment, I gained different skills. I learned how to program with HTML:5 and CSS3 and how to design difrrent layouts with flexbox, grid and layout principles such as: alignment, golden section, symmetry/asymmetry. I learned how to make a productive desk research by collecting articles, image assets and more. I learned how to use photoshop by creating a moodboard of a given art style and illustrator by designing logo and icons.
